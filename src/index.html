⚠ Before You Connect

Narciso is peer-to-peer. There are no servers between you and the people you connect with.

This means anyone you chat with can see your real IP address unless you are using a VPN. From an IP address, someone could determine your approximate location, your ISP, and potentially more.

We strongly recommend using a VPN before connecting. Narciso has a built-in VPN kill switch (⚙ Settings) that will protect you if your VPN drops.

By continuing, you acknowledge that you understand the risks of peer-to-peer communication without a VPN.
